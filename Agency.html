Why Should Agencies Offer a Data Privacy Solution?

Data privacy is top of mind for consumers and the regulatory burden is placed on businesses to protect their data. If an agency creates, hosts, or manages a website for any client, chances are the website or app is not doing what is required to protect visitor’s privacy. Offering a comprehensive solution will limit their liability from regulatory fines and also build increased engagement and trust with your client’s visitors.

How Does a Privacy Policy Generator Benefit an Agency?

By streamlining the process of generating customized, legally vetted policies, an Agency can decrease client involvement subsequently decreasing overall project timelines. This saves time and money which ultimately increases profit margins. In addition, an Agency adds a valued service that normally becomes a burden on both the agency and client.

How Does a Consent Manager Benefit an Agency?

Consumers have come to expect options to opt-in to being tracked. If a website or app does not have the ability to provide these options, consumers will lose trust, thus affecting website metrics, conversion rates, and ultimately having a negative impact on advertising and customer acquisition. By placing a customizable and elegant solution, websites can provide consent options for visitors, building trust throughout their visit.

Is an Agency Liable if They Provide a Data Privacy Compliance Solution?

No. The generation of a policy or management of consent is still the responsibility of the business. By providing an easy to use tool and solutions, agencies can help businesses avoid regulatory liability, and keep them compliant.
